Seven Falls

One of Colorado's natural wonders. Seven waterfalls cascading into one another. Truly beautiful site to behold!

About Seven Falls

The history of Seven Falls dates back to the late 1800s when Nathaniel Colby, a pioneer, discovered the falls and was captivated by their beauty. The property was later developed by James Hull, who built a series of steps and pathways to make the falls accessible to the public. Over the years, Seven Falls has been carefully preserved and maintained, allowing visitors to experience the natural wonder in its pristine form. Today, it is managed by the Broadmoor, a luxury resort that offers additional amenities and services to enhance the visitor experience.

Whether you're an avid hiker, a nature lover, or simply seeking a memorable outdoor experience, Seven Falls is a must-visit destination in Colorado. Its stunning waterfalls, breathtaking scenery, and diverse recreational opportunities make it a true gem of the Rocky Mountains.

Things to do

Hiking

What better way to enjoy nature than a nature walk? There is plenty to see here. From the green forrests, cliffs, rivers, and of course the famous seven waterfalls!

Enjoy the Scenery

Are you a nature lover? Or do you want to just simply get away from the noise of city life? Well Seven Falls can help you reach inner peace with its wonderfull scenery. Stay at the Broadmoor, where luxury meets nature!

Ziplining

Are you an adrenaline junkie? Do you like to feel the rush from zooming across the air with a beautiful sky view of nature? Near Seven Falls is one of the world's top ziplines!
